Typography

Editorial elegance meets everyday clarity

Headlines
DM Serif Display
Elegant serif for display text. Brings warmth and sophistication to headings, amounts, and key moments.
Weights: Regular, Italic  •  Use for: Titles, amounts, section headers
Body & UI
Outfit
Clean, modern sans-serif for everything else. Light weight for body text, medium for labels, semibold for emphasis.
Weights: 300, 400, 500, 600, 700  •  Use for: Body, buttons, labels, inputs

Design Principles

The rules we design by

Generous Space
Let elements breathe. White space is not wasted space — it's calm.
Soft & Rounded
Generous border radius everywhere. Nothing sharp. Everything feels safe.
Clear Hierarchy
One thing matters most on every screen. Guide the eye with intention.
Graceful Motion
Slow, elegant transitions. Nothing jarring. Everything flows naturally.
