3 Brand Directions

Finny Identity

Three distinct visual directions for a couples budgeting app that feels premium, intentional, and delightful to use.

01
F

finny

Money, together

Where couples build wealth
Dark, sophisticated, and premium. Inspired by Dugout League's dark theme aesthetic. Ember orange accents create warmth in a sleek interface. For couples who want their financial tool to feel as refined as the life they're building.

Color System

Ember #F97316
Midnight #0A0A0B
Charcoal #18181B
Success #22C55E

Typography

Headlines — Sora Bold
Your money, your rules
Body — Outfit Regular
Clean, geometric sans-serif that's highly legible on dark backgrounds. Numbers feel modern and precise.

Voice & Tone

Confident & Direct

"You're ₹12,400 ahead this month. Keep it up."

Smart Partner

"Moving ₹5,000 to savings? That's ₹60,000 by year end."

Understated Celebration

"Goal reached. Time for the next one."

Design Principles

Depth & Layers

Cards float above surfaces. Subtle shadows create hierarchy.

Restrained Color

Orange appears only where action matters. Everything else recedes.

Data-Dense

Information-rich screens that don't feel cluttered.

Micro-animations

Subtle transitions. Numbers that count up. Progress that flows.

02
F

Finny

Mindful money for two

The gentle path to financial peace
Light, warm, and grounded. Cream backgrounds with sage green accents. Serif headlines bring editorial elegance. For couples who see budgeting as self-care, not restriction. Feels like a calm morning with coffee.

Color System

Sage #10B981
Cream #FAF9F7
Stone #1C1917
Sand #D6CFC4

Typography

Headlines — Fraunces Semibold
Grow together, gently
Body — Outfit Light
Airy and approachable. The serif-sans pairing feels like a lifestyle magazine—aspirational yet accessible.

Voice & Tone

Calm & Reassuring

"You're doing beautifully. Every small step counts."

Thoughtful Guide

"This month, you've been more intentional. Notice that."

Gentle Nudge

"Want to check in on your groceries budget together?"

Design Principles

Generous Whitespace

Let elements breathe. No cramped screens. Peace in the layout.

Organic Shapes

Rounded corners everywhere. Soft, natural feeling.

Editorial Layout

Magazine-inspired grids. Content feels curated, not cluttered.

Slow Transitions

Graceful animations. Nothing jarring. Everything flows.

03
F

FINNY

Couple goals, crushed

Where partners become power couples
Bold, modern, and achievement-focused. Deep indigo with electric purple accents. For ambitious couples who track finances like they track fitness goals. Gamified, motivating, and unapologetically goal-driven.

Color System

Indigo #818CF8
Deep Navy #0F0F1A
Violet #A78BFA
Mint #34D399

Typography

Headlines — Manrope ExtraBold
LEVEL UP YOUR FINANCES
Body — Inter Regular
Clean and technical. Perfect for stats, streaks, and achievements. Feels like a premium fitness app for money.

Voice & Tone

Hype Coach

"14-day streak! You two are unstoppable."

Achievement Unlocked

"New badge: First ₹50K saved together."

Competitive Edge

"You're in the top 10% of savers this month."

Design Principles

Gradient Energy

Purple-to-indigo gradients create motion and excitement.

Stats & Streaks

Numbers front and center. Progress rings. Achievement cards.

Bold Typography

Large, confident text. All-caps for emphasis. Impact.

Celebratory Motion

Confetti on milestones. Pulse animations. Rewarding feedback.
